About Me

My name is James (Jim) Clark. I currently work at a digital marketing agency in New Hampshire doing Search Engine Optimization for local businesses. We work with businesses in any industry ranging from restaurants to data storage companies. SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of increasing “organic search traffic” to a certain website. The process is broken up into several phases.

  • Keyword Research
  • Competitor Analysis
  • Website Audit
  • My Business Optimization
  • On-Page SEO
  • Link Building & Off-Page SEO

Additional to my passion for SEO, I also create search engine friendly websites for local businesses on my own. I’ve created websites, handled social media & SEO for “Pro Kitchens & Trim” and “Heavenly Foods Direct. Thank you for reading about me & I hope you enjoy the rest of my portfolio.
